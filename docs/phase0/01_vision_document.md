# LEAG FX — Vision Document

**Phase 0 / Step 1**

---

## What LEAG FX Is

LEAG FX is an AI Operating System that researches, understands, explains, validates, simulates, and executes decisions for the USD/JPY currency pair — under strict risk governance.

It behaves like an institutional research desk: it observes the market, collects and validates reliable data, researches statistically, engineers meaningful features, models patterns with machine learning, backtests every idea, analyzes with specialized AI experts, reaches consensus using evidence, applies strict risk governance, executes cautiously, remembers every decision, and reflects to improve — explaining every recommendation in plain language.

## What LEAG FX Is Not

- It is **not** a signal-selling bot or a black box that spits out "buy/sell" with no reasoning.
- It is **not** designed to guarantee profitable trades — no system can honestly promise that.
- It is **not** built to trade many currency pairs. It stays narrow, on USD/JPY, until the core system is proven.
- It is **not** allowed to execute real trades without passing through simulation, paper trading, and demo stages first, and never bypassing risk governance.

## Who It's For

Right now: one builder (Gbengisto), learning and building at expert level in data science, ML, and AI engineering. LEAG FX exists to be both:
1. A genuinely working research and decision-support system for USD/JPY.
2. A flagship, portfolio-quality demonstration of institutional-grade AI/ML engineering practice.

## Why It Exists

Most retail trading tools skip the hard, unglamorous parts — data validation, bias detection, honest backtesting, risk governance, auditability — and jump straight to a prediction model. That's why most of them fail quietly. LEAG FX exists to prove that a solo builder can do this the way an institution would: disciplined, explainable, and evidence-based at every step.

## Guiding Principle

**Transparency and auditability over speed.** Every recommendation LEAG FX makes must be explainable in plain language, backed by evidence, and traceable back through the system that produced it. If a decision can't be explained, it isn't trusted — no matter how confident the model is.

## Definition of Success (Phase 0 level)

LEAG FX is succeeding if, at every phase, it produces something real, working, and honest — even if the honest answer at some point is "this doesn't beat the baseline, and that's valuable to know."
